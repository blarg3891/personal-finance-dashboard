# Privacy Policy for Beeron Tech Dashboard

**Last Updated:** December 25, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Beeron Tech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Beeron Tech Dashboard application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information retrieved through our integrations.

## 2. Data Collection
This application retrieves financial data (holdings, balances, and transactions) solely for the purpose of personal portfolio tracking and analysis.

* **Data Source:** We use the SnapTrade API to connect to brokerage accounts.
* **Data Storage:** Data retrieved is stored locally on the user's secure server/machine.

## 3. Data Usage
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. All data is used strictly for generating personal financial reports.

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via the information provided in our repository README.
